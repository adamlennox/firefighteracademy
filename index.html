The Reality

WHY MOST CANDIDATES FAIL

Ontario firefighter aptitude tests are tough by design. They're built to eliminate hundreds — sometimes thousands — of applicants at once.

  • Lack of structured preparation before test day
  • Fast, unforgiving time limits that catch people off guard
  • Math problems you haven't seen since high school
  • Mechanical reasoning that feels completely foreign
  • Human relations judgment questions with no clear right answer
  • Maps, diagrams, and spatial awareness under pressure
